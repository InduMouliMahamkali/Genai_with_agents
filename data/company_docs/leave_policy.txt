Company Leave Policy

1. Employees are entitled to 20 paid leaves per year.
2. Leaves can be carried forward up to 5 days into the next year.
3. Sick leave does not require manager approval for up to 2 consecutive days.
4. For planned vacation leaves, prior approval from the reporting manager is required.
5. Maternity leave: 6 months | Paternity leave: 15 days
6. Leaves can be applied via the HR portal or email to hr@company.com.
