Welcome to the company! This handbook is designed to help you understand our culture, policies, and expectations.

- **Work Hours**: Standard work hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST, Monday to Friday.
- **Leave Policy**: Full-time employees are entitled to 24 annual leave days, in addition to national holidays.
- **Remote Work**: We support hybrid and remote work. Coordinate with your manager for regular check-ins and task tracking.
- **Code of Conduct**: We expect professional, respectful, and inclusive behavior from all employees, both in-person and online.
- **Performance Reviews**: Bi-annual performance cycles are held in April and October, with clear KPIs and 360-degree feedback.

Please reach out to HR at hr@example.com if you have any questions.
