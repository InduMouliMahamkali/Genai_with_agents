New Employee Onboarding Guide

1. Day 1: Welcome email and IT setup
2. Day 2: Intro sessions with HR, Tech, and PM teams
3. Week 1: Mandatory compliance and security training
4. Week 2: Buddy assigned and role-specific mentoring begins
5. Laptop, ID card, and system access are arranged by IT within 24 hours of joining
6. Use the onboarding Slack channel for any help during your first month
